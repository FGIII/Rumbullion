HUNKY DORY

By David Bowie

Released 1971

Hunky Dory is yet another departure from the ever-experimenting Bowie.
This album is truly eclectic and etheral, with such offerings as
Andy Warhol, Song for Bob Dylan, Life on Mars and the senseless but
magisterial, Bewlay Brothers. A true epic.

The Band

  • David Bowie - Vocals
  • Mick Ronson - Guitars
  • Trevor Bolder - Bass
  • Mick Woodmansey - Drums
  • Rick Wakeman - Piano
